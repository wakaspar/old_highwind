About Me

My name is Will Kaspar and I'm a web developer based out of San Francisco, California. I've been coding since 2016, working in both JavaScript and Ruby. In that time, I've managed to learn a handful of other useful fullstack technologies, including Node.js, Express, Angular.js, Rails, and SaSS, to name a handful. A more comprehensive list can be found below.

I'm very passionate about developing software - it gives me a wonderful outlet for my creativity while allowing me to pursue my interests in technology. Web development demands the perfect balance of design and technology to pique my interest. Hopefully, as you explore this website, that passion comes through :)

When I'm not building software, I'm passionate about the outdoors. I grew up in southeastern Tennessee and absolutely adore the mountains. I thoroughly enjoy hiking, camping, and backpacking. In a past life, I worked as a residential gardener and I still keep a garden today. I have a dog, Indigo, who's a 5-year-old cattle beagle mix and my most loyal companion. I also love pinball and all kinds of gaming. Other interests include game development, computer security, and history.

My favorite food is pepperoni pizza.
